ClawdView Portfolios

Strategic portfolio management for enterprise leaders. Align investments with business outcomes, prioritize the right work, and deliver measurable value across your entire portfolio.

Overview

ClawdView Portfolios is the strategic layer of the ClawdView platform, designed for C-suite executives, PMO leaders, and portfolio managers who need to make data-driven investment decisions across hundreds of projects, programs, and products. It provides a single pane of glass for understanding where your organization is investing, what's delivering value, and where resources are being wasted.

Unlike project-level tools that focus on task execution, Portfolios operates at the strategic investment level — answering questions like "Should we fund Initiative A or Initiative B?" and "Which business capability has the highest risk of under-delivery?"

Core Features

Investment Analysis

Compare funding scenarios with what-if modeling. Evaluate ROI, NPV, and payback period across portfolio items.

Strategic Roadmapping

Create multi-year roadmaps aligned to business capabilities. Drag-and-drop timeline planning with dependency visualization.

Capacity Planning

Model resource demand vs. supply across teams and time horizons. Identify bottlenecks before they impact delivery.

Scoring & Prioritization

Define custom scoring models with weighted criteria. Rank initiatives objectively using configurable frameworks.

Executive Dashboards

Pre-built and customizable dashboards for board presentations. Real-time health indicators, risk heat maps, and trend analysis.

Stage-Gate Governance

Configure approval workflows for initiative lifecycle. Define gate criteria, reviewers, and escalation paths.

⚠ Scaling Limitation

Portfolio views with more than 2,000 items may experience rendering delays of 3-5 seconds on the roadmap timeline view. For portfolios exceeding this threshold, we recommend using filtered views or the Portfolio Groups feature to segment content. See the Enterprise Scaling Guide for optimization strategies.

Integration Points

Portfolios connects upstream and downstream through ClawdView Hub:

  • Upstream (Strategy): Import strategic plans from PowerPoint, Excel, or OKR platforms (Gtmhub, WorkBoard)
  • Downstream (Execution): Automatic status roll-up from PPM Pro projects and AgilePlace boards
  • Financial: Bidirectional sync with SAP, Oracle Financials, and NetSuite for budget actuals
  • Reporting: Export to Power BI, Tableau, and Looker via the Analytics API

Common Use Cases

IT Portfolio Rationalization

Use the Scoring & Prioritization module to evaluate your application portfolio against criteria like business value, technical debt, TCO, and strategic alignment. The built-in rationalization templates help identify candidates for retirement, consolidation, or investment — typically reducing application portfolio costs by 15-25% in the first year.

M&A Integration Planning

During mergers and acquisitions, Portfolios provides a unified view of both organizations' project portfolios. The "Portfolio Comparison" feature highlights overlapping investments, conflicting priorities, and integration synergy opportunities. Capacity planning models help estimate the combined organization's ability to absorb integration workload alongside business-as-usual delivery.

Annual Planning Cycle

Streamline the annual budgeting and planning process with collaborative scenario planning. Business unit leaders submit initiative proposals through configurable intake forms, which are automatically scored and ranked. Portfolio review committees use the Governance module to approve, defer, or reject proposals with full audit trails.

💡 Best Practice

Start with no more than 5-7 scoring criteria when implementing prioritization. Organizations that use more than 10 criteria tend to see reduced stakeholder engagement and decision fatigue. You can always refine your model after the first planning cycle. See Portfolio Governance Framework for detailed guidance.
