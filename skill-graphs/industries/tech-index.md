# Tech Sector Industry Guide

Software and AI companies are typically fast-moving, highly technical, and quick to adopt (or abandon) new tools.

## Key Themes
- **Agile Maturity:** They often already practice Agile natively (Jira, GitHub), but lack visibility at the team-of-teams level. This makes them ideal candidates for [[planview-agileplace]].
- **Tool Sprawl:** They suffer from having too many disconnected tools.
- **Multi-Cloud Architecture:** They often expect SaaS providers to run across multiple clouds (AWS, GCP) for redundancy.

## Value Drivers
- Time-to-market for new software features.
- Bridging the gap between engineering execution and executive strategy.
