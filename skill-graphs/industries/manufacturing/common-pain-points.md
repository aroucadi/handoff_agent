# Manufacturing Pain Points

## 1. The Quote Speed Problem

Manufacturing sales cycles are long (6–18 months), but individual quote requests must be fast. The paradox: complex products require expert configuration, but customers demand 24-hour turnarounds. Most manufacturing companies average **2–3 weeks** for custom quotes. Competitors who respond faster win.

## 2. The Knowledge Silo Problem

Pricing and configuration knowledge lives in the heads of 2–3 senior sales engineers. When they're on vacation, sick, or leave the company, the entire quote process stops. VeloSaaS CPQ captures this tribal knowledge in code.

## 3. The Margin Erosion Problem

Without systematic pricing governance, field sales reps negotiate ad-hoc discounts. Manufacturing margins are already thin (15–25%). Uncontrolled discounting can push deals below breakeven without anyone noticing until quarterly results.

## 4. The ERP Disconnect Problem

Quotes are created in Excel or legacy tools, then manually re-entered into the ERP. This double-entry causes: pricing errors (avg. 8% error rate), order delays (3–5 days), and customer frustration.

## What CSMs Should Know

These pain points are **why they bought**. Reference them in kickoff calls to build credibility. When the champion hears you understand their world, trust accelerates. When stakeholders see you connecting their specific pain to VeloSaaS's specific capability, they believe the implementation will succeed.
