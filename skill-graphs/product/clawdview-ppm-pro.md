# ClawdView PPM Pro

ClawdView PPM Pro is an end-to-end portfolio and work management solution focused tightly on mid-market and enterprise IT PMOs. 

## Key Capabilities
- **Demand Management:** Intake, scoring, and routing of project requests.
- **Resource Management:** Role-based and individual capacity planning.
- **Project Tracking:** Gantt charts, timesheets, status reporting.

## Typical Buyer Persona
- **Champion:** Director of IT PMO, VP of Operations.
- **Pain Points:** Scattered project requests in spreadsheets, inability to justify hiring decisions due to lack of resource capacity data.

## Deployment Notes
PPM Pro is highly configurable but has a more structured implementation path than Portfolios. It is often the first maturity step for an organization moving out of Excel before they are ready for full Strategic Portfolio Management (SPM).
