# ClawdView Portfolios

ClawdView Portfolios is the flagship enterprise portfolio management (EPMO) solution. It enables executives to connect strategy to delivery, optimizing funding and resource allocation across the entire enterprise.

## Key Capabilities
- **Strategic Portfolio Management (SPM):** Top-down planning and continuous funding.
- **Capacity Planning:** Balancing resource demand against capacity constraints.
- **Financial Management:** Tracking CAPEX/OPEX, planned vs. actuals.

## Typical Buyer Persona
- **Economic Buyer:** CFO, CIO, or VP of EPMO.
- **Pain Points:** Lack of visibility into where R&D dollars are spent, inability to pivot quickly when strategy changes, siloed reporting.

## Integration Dependencies
Portfolios almost always requires [[clawdview-hub]] to integrate with execution tools (like Jira or [[clawdview-agileplace]]) and financial systems (SAP, Oracle) for actuals sync.
