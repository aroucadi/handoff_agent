# VeloSaaS — Product Knowledge

VeloSaaS is an enterprise CRM and revenue operations platform serving mid-market and enterprise B2B companies. Founded in 2018, it has grown to 5,000+ customers across manufacturing, financial services, technology, and healthcare verticals.

## Core Modules

- [[cpq-module]] — Configure, Price, Quote engine for complex product catalogs. This is VeloSaaS's flagship product and the #1 reason companies buy.
- [[revenue-cloud]] — Revenue recognition, subscription management, and billing automation. Often sold alongside CPQ.

- [[common-failure-modes]] — What goes wrong and why, based on post-mortems
- [[integration-gotchas]] — ERP, CRM, and third-party integration pitfalls

## Key Differentiators

1. **Native AI quoting** — Gemini-powered price optimization suggests optimal discounting based on deal history
2. **Real-time revenue intelligence** — Live dashboards for CFOs showing forecast accuracy
3. **Vertical-specific templates** — Pre-built configurations for manufacturing, financial services, and technology
